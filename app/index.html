Purchase_request

The software helps big organizations to buy and sell equipment.
The target users are the employees who use the equipment to run the organization's business.

Ordering

Here the user is presented a form that the user can use to key in the equipment need plus the specifications.

Notification

Both the invoice to purchase the above equipment and the required approval from an administrator can be obtained through this system.

Ratings

This part of the system helps us to rate the vendors so that the administration can easily pick the preferred vendors due to an algorithm for their efficiency.

About

When I worked at Siginon organization, I discovered that despite it being the biggest and probably one of the most advanced organizations in Kenya, it still had delays in purchasing and delivering equipment that affected the day-to-day running of things. You can maintain equipment past its lifespan, but you cannot maintain it forever, and if pushing the equipment past its lifespan is also inefficient. When the equipment finally gives up and it takes a week to request and order the equipment, it really holds up the work the equipment was doing. So, the above system orders for you once the purchase has been approved.
